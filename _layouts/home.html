    • Biography

    I am a Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Connecticut. I obtained my B.S. degree from Queen Mary University of London and B.A. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2016 and M.S. degree from University of Texas at Dallas in 2018.

    My research interests include Security & Privacy in Machine Learning (e.g., Gradient Attack, Reconstruction Attack), Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning (e.g., Differential Privacy, MPC and TEE) and Efficient AI(e.g., Weight Pruning and Quantization).
